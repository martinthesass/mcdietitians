Frequesntly Asked Questions

How do I book a consultation?

The best way to book a consultation is through email or via whatsapp. Simply send us a message telling us what you need help with or request more information. If we can help we will book a consultation with you. Simple. If we feel we are not the right for you we can still provide information on where to go next. We won't charge for this.

Why don't you offer one off consultations for weight loss?

The reason we don't offer one off appointments for weight loss clients is that the change that is needed takes time. It is not simply a matter of following a diet and hoping for the best. Adjustments will be needed week by week to ensure progress.You need to change who you are as a person and this won't happen in one session. We don't feel we can provide any value from one session and we want to provide the best service and value we can. We feel one session would be doing you a great diservice.

I am sick of my diet it is boring can you help?

In short no. We provide information that you will need to put in place yourself. We are not chef's and cannot provide recipes.

I don't go to the gym can I still lose weight?

Yes you can but your results will likely be limited. If your not at the stage where you are able to go to the gym thats fine. We can provide information to help you get started (we won't charge you) but to see a real change we have very detailed and structured programmes for this and you are more than welcome to come back when you feel you are ready.

I have never used the gym before I don't even know where to start!

Don't worry we will guide you through step by step.

I just want to lose weight or fix my IBS why do I have to fill out the questionnaire?

Our business depends on results. We don't simply throw a diet at you and hope for the best. We need to understand everything about you sleep, social life, work patterns, stress among others. This way we can design the right programme for you.
